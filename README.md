# POKéMON Crystal Encounters .txt for quick reference

These are simple .txt files containing Pokémon Crystal encounters by species and by locations.

Useful for quick referencing Pokémon encounters directly on your handheld console instead of looking everything up on your phone/PC.

I made this so I could read it using the Ebook Reader on my Miyoo Mini Plus, so I didn't have to check my phone every time I wanted to look up a Pokémons' location.
